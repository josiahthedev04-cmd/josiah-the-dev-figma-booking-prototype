# Booking Flow

## Purpose

The booking flow helps a customer move from interest to action.

The customer can view services, choose a package, enter project details, review the request and submit the booking.

## Desktop Booking Flow

Home → Services → Service Details → Packages → Booking Form → Review Booking → Booking Confirmation

## Mobile Booking Flow

Splash Screen → Home → Services → Service Details → Packages → Booking Form → Review Booking → Booking Confirmation

## Step 1: Home

The customer lands on the home screen and understands what Josiah The Dev Digital Services offers.

Main goal:

Help the customer start the booking journey.

Main actions:

- Book a Service
- View Services

## Step 2: Services

The customer views the available services.

Services included:

- AI Support
- Coding
- Web Development
- Social Media Management
- Graphic Design
- Video Editing

Main goal:

Help the customer choose the type of support they need.

## Step 3: Service Details

The customer opens a service and reads more details.

Example service:

AI Support

Main goal:

Help the customer understand what the service includes.

## Step 4: Packages

The customer compares the available packages.

Packages included:

- Starter Audit
- Business Launch Kit
- Content Growth Support
- Custom AI/Web Build

Main goal:

Help the customer choose the best package for their project.

## Step 5: Booking Form

The customer enters their project details.

Information collected:

- Name
- Business name
- Email
- Phone or WhatsApp
- Service needed
- Package selected
- Budget range
- Project timeline
- Project details

Main goal:

Collect enough information to understand the customer's request.

## Step 6: Review Booking

The customer checks the booking details before submitting.

Main goal:

Reduce mistakes and give the customer confidence before sending the request.

## Step 7: Booking Confirmation

The customer sees a success message after submitting the request.

Main goal:

Confirm that the request was received and explain what happens next.

## Business Value

This flow helps a digital services business collect better client leads.

It makes the process simple for the customer and gives the business the information needed to follow up.
