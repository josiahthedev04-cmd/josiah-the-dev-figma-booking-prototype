# Prototype Summary

## Project Name

Josiah The Dev Digital Services Booking Prototype

## Project Type

Figma website and mobile booking prototype

## Live Prototype

https://crane-nap-40239792.figma.site/

## Business Idea

Josiah The Dev Digital Services is a digital services business that helps clients with AI support, coding, web development, social media management, graphic design and video editing.

## Problem

Many small businesses and clients need digital help but do not always know what service to ask for. They also need a simple way to view services, compare packages and send a booking request.

## Solution

This prototype gives customers a simple booking experience.

The customer can:

1. Visit the home page
2. View the available services
3. Choose a service
4. Select a package
5. Complete a booking form
6. Review the request
7. Submit the booking
8. Receive confirmation

## Target Users

This prototype is designed for:

- Small business owners
- Local service providers
- Content creators
- Freelancers
- Startups
- Clients who need digital support

The main goal is to show a real business booking flow that can help turn visitors into client leads.
